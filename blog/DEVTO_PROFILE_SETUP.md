## Step 6: Publish Your Posts!
Copy the blog posts from the `blog/` folder:
1. `post-1-ai-portfolio.md` → Your flagship post
2. `post-2-aeronautics-to-devops.md` → Your career story
3. `post-3-devops-portfolio-guide.md` → Technical guide

### How to Publish:
1. Go to https://dev.to/new
2. Paste the content from each .md file
3. The front matter (---) at the top sets the title, tags, and cover image
4. Click "Publish" 🚀
