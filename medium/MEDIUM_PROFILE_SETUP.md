## 6. Medium-Specific Writing Tips

### Titles That Earn Money
```
✅ "The $3,000/Month Kubernetes Mistake Nobody Talks About"
✅ "I Reviewed 200 DevOps Resumes — Here's What Gets Hired"
✅ "Why Your CI/CD Pipeline Is Secretly Costing You $50K/Year"
✅ "The One Kubernetes Config That Prevented a Production Breach"

❌ "Introduction to Kubernetes Cost Optimization"
❌ "A Guide to CI/CD Best Practices"
❌ "How to Write a DevOps Resume"
```

### Formatting for Maximum Read Time (= more income)
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
- Subheadings every 200-300 words
- Bold key insights
- Use blockquotes for important callouts
- Include 1-2 images/diagrams
- End with a strong CTA (follow, clap, comment)

### Optimal Post Length
- Sweet spot: 7-12 minute read (1,800-3,000 words)
- Longer = more read time = more money
- But don't pad — every sentence must earn its place
